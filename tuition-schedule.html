Registration & Reservation Policy

Registration fees are deducted from the total tuition cost. They secure your seat in the class and cover initial administrative processing.

  • Non-Refundable: Registration fees are non-refundable but are consumable/transferable to the next session.
  • Inclusions: Varies by program (see table below).
