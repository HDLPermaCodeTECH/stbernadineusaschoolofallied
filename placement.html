How It Works

Your Journey to the USA

A transparent, step-by-step process designed for your success.

1

Document Screening

The applicant must send all the required documents and credentials for initial screening, including a resume, academic transcripts and diploma, license and certifications, professional experience, and specific skills needed by the employer.

2

Legal Review

Our immigration attorney will review all the applicant documents, employer's business documents, and job requirements to see if the foreign health worker qualifies.

3

Visa Petition

Submission of pre-filing requirements to DOL, followed by petition filing with DHS.

4

Green Card Application

Green card application through adjustment of status if the applicant is in the US or consular processing if the foreign professional is not in the US.

5

Onboarding

Visa processing assistance at US Consulate (if applicable). The employee will then receive a briefing on their rights and obligations as a foreign worker in the US.
