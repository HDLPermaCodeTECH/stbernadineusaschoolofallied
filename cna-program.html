Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)

Start your healthcare career with our state-approved CNA training program.

Program Overview

Our CNA program is a comprehensive 90-hour course (50 hours classroom and 40 hours clinicals) designed to provide you with essential skills for safe, compassionate patient care.

We offer flexible Day and Evening sessions to accommodate your schedule. Please note that slots are limited and fill up quickly.

Requirements

  • High School Level
  • State ID / Driver's License
  • Social Security Card
  • X-Ray / PPD / Quantiferon
  • Covid Vaccine Record

Program Details

Duration: 90 Hours (50 Classroom / 40 Clinical)

Schedule: Day and Evening classes offered

All inclusive of books, registration for the licensure exam, and 2 additional uniform scrubs.

Registration: $500

($200 Non-refundable Reservation fee)
