Certified Medication Aide (CMA)

Advance your nursing assistant career with specialized medication administration training.

Program Overview

Our CMA program is a 56-hour specialized course: 40 hours classroom and 16 hours of clinical/medication pass in an assisted living facility under nurse supervision.

This program is designed for current CHHAs or CNAs looking to expand their clinical capabilities.

Requirements

  • Pre-requisite: CHHA or CNA License
  • High School Diploma or GED
  • State ID / Driver's License
  • Social Security Card

Program Details

Total Hours: 56 Hours

Clinical Focus: Medication Pass in Assisted Living

Supervision: Registered Nurse (RN) Oversight

For CNA & CMA students, fees are all inclusive of books, and registration for the licensure exam, with 2 additional uniform scrubs.

Non-refundable Reservation: $200*
