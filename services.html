Certification Programs

Certified Nurse Aide (CNA)

90 hours (50 hours classroom and 40 hours clinicals). Prepare for state certification with hands-on training.

  • Schedule: Day and Evening
  • Outcome: State Licensure
  • Limited slots available
Learn More

Certified Homemaker Home Health Aide (CH-HHA)

76 hours (60 hours classroom and 16 hours of evaluated skills). Ideal for home health careers.

  • Schedule: Weekdays/Weekends
  • Outcome: NJ State Certification
  • Requirement: High School Level
Learn More

Certified Medication Aide (CMA)

56 hours including clinical medication pass in an assisted living facility under nurse supervision.

  • Pre-requisite: CHHA or CNA
  • Outcome: State Certification
  • Clinical Supervision included
Learn More

Patient Care Technician (PCT)

220 hours (5 Months including externship). Advanced skills for hospital and clinic settings.

  • Includes Externship
  • High School Diploma/GED required
  • Extensive clinical focus
Learn More

Certified Medical Assistant

620 hours (5 months training plus externship). Comprehensive clinical and administrative training.

  • Externship included
  • All-inclusive materials
  • Full career preparation
