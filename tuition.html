Fee Policies & Inclusions

  • * Reservation fees are strictly non-refundable but are consumable for the next session of your chosen program.

  • * For PCT and Medical Assistant Program, fees are books and 2 set of uniform scrubs.
  • * For CH-HHA and CPR, the amount is inclusive of the book only.

Payment Plans

We understand that tuition can be a significant investment. St. Bernadine School of Allied Health, Healthcare Services offers flexible weekly or bi-weekly payment plans with no interest.

Note: Full tuition must be paid prior to graduation to receive your certificate.
