Admissions Process

Your step-by-step guide to becoming a student at St. Bernadine.

1. Fill Out & Submit

Directly fill out the form online or download the PDF to submit in person or via email.

2. Wait for Our Call

Once submitted, our admissions team will contact you to confirm details and approve enrollment.

3. Pay & Register

Pay the registration fee securely online, over the phone, or in our office to secure your spot.

4. Confirmation

Two weeks before class starts, we'll send your finalized schedule via email or text.

5. Orientation

Attend orientation one week prior to class to receive your books, ID, and materials.
